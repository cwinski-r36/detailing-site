About Us

Our Story

Flatiron Detail Co started with a simple idea: your car deserves better than a drive-through rinse. We're two friends from Boulder who turned a passion for clean cars into a hands-on detailing business. Every job we take on, we treat like it's our own vehicle.

What We Do

We offer hand car washes, full interior and exterior details, pet hair removal, and trash can cleaning. We bring our supplies to you — no drop-off needed.

Our Values

We believe in showing up on time, being honest about pricing, and never cutting corners.
